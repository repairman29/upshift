Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card for Free tier?

No. The Free tier is free forever and includes unlimited local CLI usage. You only pay if you need high-volume AI features.

What counts as an AI query?

Conversational questions to JARVIS ("analyze my deps"), smart risk assessments, and AI-powered suggestions count. Basic CLI commands do not count.

Can I use this for commercial projects?

Yes! UpshiftAI is open source (MIT) and free for commercial use. You only pay for hosted AI cloud features.

How does JARVIS integration work?

Install the skill in your CLAWDBOT agent. Set your API key from the dashboard to unlock conversational analysis.
